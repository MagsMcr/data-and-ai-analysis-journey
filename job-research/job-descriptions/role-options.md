5 UK EdTech Companies:
1. Century (Century Tech) - @ThisIsCentury

Cloud-based AI platform for personalized learning
Automates homework setting and marking
Provides data on student struggles for tailored lesson planning
Website: century.tech

2. Lexplore - @LexploreUK

Swedish startup using AI + eye-tracking technology for reading assessment
Identifies reading ability gaps and potential dyslexia
Cloud-based dashboard for teachers
Addresses UK literacy crisis (1 in 5 children can't read well by age 11)
Website: lexplore.co.uk

3. Peergrade - @Peergrade

Peer feedback platform
Students give/receive feedback beyond just teacher input
Teacher dashboard shows feedback quality, rubric data, evaluation insights
Already used globally (New Zealand to Denmark)
Website: peergrade.io

4. Firefly - @FireFlyTeam

All-in-one teacher portal (lesson plans, resources, timetables, homework)
Biggest Series A funding for UK EdTech (2016)
2/3 of UK independent schools use it
95% teacher adoption rate, 96% school retention
Website: fireflylearning.com

5. Mondly - @MondlyLanguages

AR/VR language learning app
Virtual chatbot native speakers for practice
Addresses UK's poor foreign language engagement
Website: mondly.com
