### 2. Student Dropout Analysis (OULAD)
**Focus:** Data joins, filtering patterns, engagement analysis  
**Dataset:** 32,593 Open University students across multiple courses  
**Tools:** Python, pandas, statistical exploration

Examined student unregistration patterns to identify:
- Timing of dropout decisions (early vs. late semester)
- Courses with highest dropout rates
- Demographic patterns among students who unregister
- Engagement differences between dropouts who submitted assessments vs. those who didn't

**Analytical approach:** Semi-joins and anti-joins to compare student cohorts, multiple sequential merges across demographic and assessment data.
